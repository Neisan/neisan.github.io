NEISAN MASSARRAT

Business Strategy, Data & Open Innovation

Neisan Massarrat is the Director of Strategic Programs at SecondMuse, working at the intersection of business strategy, data and open innovation to solve complex challenges. He has over 10 years of experience developing business innovation programs for some of the world's leading development and technology organizations, including Intel Corporation, NASA, Nike, NYCEDC, USAID and The World Bank Group.

His areas of expertise include big data, corporate strategy, startup acceleration, public-private partnerships and program management. Over the past four years, Neisan has developed data-driven programs for the World Bank focused on tackling economic development issues in areas such as citizen engagement, climate change, disaster risk management and urban planning.

Before SecondMuse, Neisan developed large scale CSR and sustainability programs across Asia at the German development agency GIZ. During his career in professional services, he has provided strategic and operational advice to both public and private sector clients at executive and ministerial levels. Neisan is based in Washington DC and has implemented projects in over 20 countries. He has a background in business management and economics.
